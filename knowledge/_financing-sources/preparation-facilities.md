# Project Preparation Facilities and Technical Assistance for NBS/Climate in Brazil

## 1. C40 Cities Finance Facility (CFF)

### Overview
Jointly implemented by C40 Cities and GIZ, the CFF helps cities prepare and deliver climate change projects by providing technical assistance to transform sustainability priorities into bankable investment proposals.

### Funding
- German Federal Ministry for Economic Cooperation and Development (BMZ)
- UK Foreign, Commonwealth and Development Office (FCDO)

### Services
- Technical assistance for project preparation
- Capacity development for municipal staff
- Access to sector experts
- Support from concept to financial close

### Brazil Activity
- **Curitiba**: Solar panel installation on four bus terminals and deactivated Caximba Landfill, with pathway to scale rooftop solar across all public buildings and bus stops
- Supports C40 member cities in Brazil (Sao Paulo, Rio de Janeiro, Salvador, Curitiba, Porto Alegre, Belo Horizonte, Recife, etc.)

### Eligibility
- C40 member cities
- Projects must be climate mitigation or adaptation focused
- Must demonstrate pathway to scale and replication

### Ticket Size (TA value)
- Typically EUR 200K-1M in technical assistance per project
- No direct capital investment; prepares projects to access other finance

## 2. GCF Project Preparation Facility (PPF)

### Overview
The PPF provides financial and technical support to develop high-quality funding proposals for submission to the Green Climate Fund.

### Funding
- Up to USD 1.5 million per PPF application
- Grants (reimbursable and non-reimbursable) and equity

### Services
- Feasibility studies
- Environmental and social impact assessments
- Financial structuring and modeling
- Legal and institutional analysis
- Gender assessment and action plans

### Access Pathway
- Submitted by GCF Accredited Entities (not directly by cities)
- Brazil AEs: BNDES, FUNBIO, Conservation International-Brazil, UNDP, World Bank
- Must be preparing a GCF funding proposal

### Relevance to Brazilian Municipalities
- Municipalities work through AEs (typically BNDES or UNDP)
- PPF funds the preparation cost of the GCF proposal itself
- Particularly useful for first-time GCF applicants

## 3. GCF Readiness and Preparatory Support Programme

### Overview
Supports country-driven initiatives to strengthen institutional capacities, governance mechanisms, and planning frameworks for climate action.

### Funding
- Up to USD 1 million per year per country
- 4-year programming cycle (improved from annual cycles)
- Non-reimbursable grants

### Services
- Strengthening National Designated Authorities (NDAs)
- Strategic frameworks for GCF engagement
- Pipeline development
- Knowledge sharing and learning

### Brazil NDA
- Ministry of Finance (Ministerio da Fazenda)
- Coordinates GCF readiness activities nationally

## 4. Global Subnational Climate Fund - TA Facility (SnCF Global)

### Overview
GCF-funded (FP151) facility specifically for subnational project preparation. USD 28 million total (USD 18.5M from GCF).

### Services
- Up to 50 pre-feasibility studies
- Up to 40 feasibility studies
- Support for 40 projects to reach financial close
- Covers solid waste, sewage, restorative agriculture, renewable energy, energy efficiency

### Target Deal Sizes
- Prepares projects for the SnCF Equity Fund (FP152)
- Target investment per project: USD 5-75 million
- Focus on subnational entities in developing countries

### Access
- Through fund managers and GCF accredited entities
- Projects screened for climate impact, financial viability, and scalability

## 5. ICLEI Transformative Actions Program (TAP)

### Overview
Global initiative to help local and regional governments transform climate concepts into bankable investment-ready projects. Represents approximately 100 projects and EUR 3 billion investment potential.

### Services
- Project incubation and mentoring
- Technical review and feedback
- Connection to project preparation facilities and funders
- Annual calls for project mobilization

### Technical Assistance Value
- EUR 100,000-500,000 per project (depends on funder)
- ICLEI does not provide capital directly; connects to finance

### Access
- Annual open calls for project submissions
- Expert screening panel
- Projects that qualify receive tailored support
- Investment-ready projects connected to financiers

### Relevance
- Strong network in Brazil through ICLEI South America (based in Sao Paulo)
- KfW partnership for urban resilience finance (extended at COP30 in Belem)

## 6. PCVR - Banco de Projetos (Project Bank)

### Overview
Brazil's federal Programa Cidades Verdes Resilientes operates a Project Bank that matches municipal climate/NBS projects with available financing sources.

### Services
- Project registration and cataloguing
- Technical qualification support
- Matchmaking with domestic and international funders
- Capacity building through Escola Virtual do Governo

### 100 Model Cities Initiative (launched April 2025)
- Structured support for 100 high-impact municipal climate actions
- Technical assistance for project preparation and financing access
- Priority for metropolitan regions and climate-vulnerable municipalities

### Access
- Register municipality through institutional commitment (termo de adesao)
- Submit projects to the Banco de Projetos
- Free of charge; coordinated by Ministry of Environment

## 7. AdaptaCidades

### Overview
Federal government initiative launched February 2025 to support states and municipalities with climate adaptation planning.

### Funding
- BRL 18 million (from GCF and other sources)

### Services
- Technical and financial resources for local/regional climate adaptation strategies
- Development of adaptation plans
- Vulnerability assessments
- Integration of adaptation into municipal planning

---

## 8. World Bank Gap Fund / City Climate Finance Gap Fund

### Overview
Supports cities in developing countries to move from climate action plans to investment-ready projects.

### Services
- Technical assistance for early-stage project preparation
- Pre-feasibility and feasibility studies
- Financial structuring support
- Upstream advisory for city governments

### Brazil Activity
- Supports PCVR (Green Resilient Cities Program) as a technical assistance partner
- Goal: PCVR becomes a "one-stop-shop" for municipal climate project demand and supply matching

### Funding Partners
- Germany (BMZ), Luxembourg, European Investment Bank

---

## 9. NUCA - Urban Climate Action Program

### Overview
Funded by Germany's International Climate Initiative (IKI), conducted globally by AFD with BNDES as the implementing partner in Brazil.

### Objective
- Strengthen institutional capacities of national development banks to promote investments in low-carbon and climate-resilient urban infrastructure

### Services
- Capacity building for BNDES staff on climate project assessment
- Development of urban climate project pipelines
- Knowledge exchange between national development banks globally

## 10. IDB Technical Cooperation Programs

### FINEP Clima (BR-T1412)
- Joint IDB-FINEP program for climate technology innovation
- Strengthens FINEP's institutional capacity for climate portfolio
- Technical cooperation grant

### ProAdapt (historical)
- IDB program for climate adaptation in Latin America
- Supported pilot adaptation projects
- Some projects active in Brazil

### IDB Lab
- Innovation laboratory of the IDB Group
- Grants and investments for early-stage climate solutions
- Typical ticket: USD 150K-2M

## 11. Conservation International - GCF Agency

### Overview
CI is a GCF Accredited Entity that can prepare and submit proposals on behalf of Brazilian projects.

### Brazil Activity
- Amazon Sustainable Landscapes Project partner
- Marajo Resiliente agroforestry project (SAP031)
- Can prepare GCF proposals for NBS projects

---

## 12. FUNBIO (Fundo Brasileiro para a Biodiversidade)

### Overview
Brazilian biodiversity fund and GCF Accredited Entity.

### Role in Project Preparation
- Can channel GCF readiness and PPF resources
- Experience managing GEF, GCF, and bilateral grant projects
- Manages Amazon Fund resources alongside BNDES
- Strong track record in NBS project preparation

## Summary: Which Facility for Which Stage?

| Project Stage | Recommended Facilities |
|---|---|
| **Concept/idea** | ICLEI TAP, PCVR Banco de Projetos, Gap Fund |
| **Pre-feasibility** | SnCF TA Facility, C40 CFF, Gap Fund |
| **Feasibility study** | GCF PPF, SnCF TA Facility, C40 CFF |
| **Financial structuring** | C40 CFF, GCF PPF, IDB TC |
| **GCF proposal development** | GCF PPF, GCF Readiness, CI/FUNBIO as AEs |
| **Capacity building** | GCF Readiness, NUCA, AdaptaCidades, PCVR |
| **Adaptation planning** | AdaptaCidades, GCF Readiness |
