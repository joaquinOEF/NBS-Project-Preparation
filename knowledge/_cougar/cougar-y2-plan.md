# COUGAR Year 2 (2026) — Porto Alegre Resilience Accelerator

## Overview
Building on 2025 mapping and mobilization, 2026 focuses on translating insights into institutional growth, financing readiness, and locally stewarded decision-making.

## Three Activity Streams

### 1. Capacity Building for Keystone Organizations
Two cohorts of grassroots organizations receive targeted technical assistance:
- **Governance & Organizational Development**: Board structures, strategic planning, accountability
- **Financial & Operational Management**: Financial tracking, grant management, compliance
- **Program Design & Scaling**: MEL systems, impact demonstration, program refinement
- **Communications & Resource Mobilization**: Storytelling, fundraising, stakeholder engagement
- Delivery: workshops, mentorship, one-on-one consulting
- Output: tailored growth plan + institutional readiness per organization

### 2. Financing Alignment
- Refine priority social issues for investment (youth employment, climate resilience, inclusive urban development, health)
- Engage public/private sector partners
- Develop blended finance and community investment fund models
- Match investment-ready orgs with funding opportunities

### 3. Community Stewardship Mechanisms
- Launch community steering committee: grassroots, funders, government, private sector
- Functions: strategic guidance, accountability, sustainability
- Emphasis on inclusivity and marginalized group representation

## Budget
€350,000 catalytic capital (OAK + Hans Wilsdorf pooled funding)

## Expected Outcomes
- 2 batches of keystone organizations with institutional readiness
- Community-validated priority social issues list
- Concrete financing pathways linking grassroots to funders
- Locally owned steering committee for ongoing investment stewardship
