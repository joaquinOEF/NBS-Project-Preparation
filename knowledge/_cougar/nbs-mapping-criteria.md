# COUGAR NBS Project Mapping Criteria

## Initial Eligibility (Must Have / Exclude)

| Criterion | Must Have | Exclude If |
|---|---|---|
| **Defined Project** | Written description, clear location, defined activities, identifiable beneficiaries | Only a concept note without site, general awareness campaign |
| **Identifiable Org** | Named organization, contact person, legal status | Informal network with no accountability, no contracting entity |
| **NbS Characteristics** | Primarily uses natural systems to address a real problem | Purely grey infrastructure, awareness-only, tech without nature |

## Project Maturity Assessment (0-3 scale)

| Metric | 0 (Lowest) | 1 | 2 | 3 (Highest) |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Problem Clarity** | Vaguely stated | Identified not quantified | Clear with local relevance | Clear with supporting data |
| **Climate/NBS Impact** | Benefits unclear | Claimed but no mechanism | Plausible NBS pathway described | Clear mechanism with regional references |
| **Solution Clarity** | Abstract, not connected to problem | Solution identified, weak link | Clear problem-solution logic, implementation plan | Coherent logic with evidence + defined activities |
| **Site Control** | No site identified | Site identified, no access | Informal permission or municipal alignment | Formal agreement or secure tenure |
| **Org Delivery Capacity** | No prior experience | Limited small project experience | Demonstrated project management | Proven multi-stakeholder/funded project track record |
| **Team Technical Experience** | No relevant experience | General environmental experience | Pilot-scale similar experience | Proven track record in this NBS type |
| **Financial Thinking** | No cost estimate | Rough budget exists | Line-item cost estimate | Clear cost structure + funding sources |
| **Community Anchoring** | No community involvement | Beneficiaries identified, not engaged | Community involved in design/implementation | Community governance or cooperative ownership |
| **Regulatory Awareness** | No awareness of permits | Aware but no action | Preliminary conversations with authorities | Permits underway or compliance plan |

## Priority Flags (2+ = high priority)
- Land tenure secure or likely secure
- Baseline environmental data exists
- Local government expressed interest
- Potential buyers/payors identified (insurers, utilities, municipality)
- Co-financing possibility identified
- Scalable beyond one site

## Exclusion Filters
- Pure advocacy projects
- Research-only academic initiatives
- Projects dependent entirely on one unconfirmed grant
- Projects with unresolved land conflict
- Projects less than 6 months old (unless already piloted)
